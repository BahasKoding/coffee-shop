Savor the Harmony of Tradition and Taste

Our coffee shop is more than a place to drink coffee; it's a sanctuary of peace where ancient Japanese aesthetics blend seamlessly with modern flavors. From the minimalistic decor to the carefully curated coffee beans, everything invites calm and relaxation.

Handcrafted Coffee in a Zen-Inspired Setting

Pour-Over

Experience the pure essence of our single-origin beans with our meticulously crafted pour-over coffee.

Matcha Latte

Indulge in the perfect balance of earthy matcha and creamy milk, a true Japanese-inspired delight.

Cold Brew

Smooth and refreshing, our cold brew is steeped for 12 hours to bring out the coffee's natural sweetness.

Espresso

Our signature espresso blend offers a rich, full-bodied flavor with notes of chocolate and caramel.

Cappuccino

A classic Italian coffee drink, our cappuccino features equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

Iced Latte

Cool down with our refreshing iced latte, made with our signature espresso and chilled milk over ice.
